Key words
Running Count:
The total value of all cards that have been dealt.
True Count:
running count adjusted for the number of decks remaining.
High-Low (Hi-Lo) System:
popular card counting system where high cards (10, Jack, Queen, King, Ace) are counted as -1, low cards (2-6) as +1, and 7-9 as 01.
Deck Penetration:
The percentage of the deck that has been dealt before shuffling.
Bet Spread:
range of bets a player makes based on the count.
Wonging:
Entering a game only when the count is favorable.
Bankroll:
A total amount of money a player has set aside for gambling.
Insurance:

Shuffle Tracking:
A more advanced technique that involves tracking groups of cards through the shuffle.
Ace Sequencing:
Predicting when an Ace will be dealt based on previous cards.
